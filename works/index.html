About Natalia

Polish-born, Milan-based, and nine years deep into a career as a designer. Patterns and illustrations are what I do for work, but they're also what I'd do anyway. Drawing has been my thing since I was a little girl filling paper with horses, flowers, and every animal I could imagine. In the late 90s, when my grandpa brought home his first PC, I discovered Microsoft Paint and never really left the screen since - just changed the software!

Outside of work, my world is shaped by the things I love: my 10-year-old ginger rescue dog Dingo (the absolute light of my life), loud music and live gigs, fantasy novels and video games I happily disappear into, travel whenever I can, and an old-school love of exchanging letters with penpals around the world, which is something I've been doing for many, many years.

If you're interested in a collaboration, contact me at hello@nataliaborzyk.com!
